Hello! I'm Emily Chen

Personal Projects

BookTrivia: Discord Bot

Discord bot that responds to the message "bookworm" using. Questions vary between multiple choice and true/false types! Developed using Java, the Javacord API, and an online trivia database.

Tools: Java, Javacord API, Git, Discord

EcoLiving: Truth or Dare? ios App to build eco-friendly habits

Want to live a more sustainable life without uprooting all of your routines? Welcome to EcoLiving, a game that helps you build more eco-friendly habits on a daily basis! Start your days with a quick eco-truth or sustainability-dare and challenge yourself to complete a dare per a day!

Tools: Swift, Xcode, Figma

SweetSuite: Java roommate bill management (in progress)

Java system that allows roommates to manage and split common costs, tracking who one owes money to, for what item, and how much. For example, if one person covers groceries for the suite, and another person covers utilities, the program will track how much each roommate owes these two individually. Working on saving a Suite's data with JDBA and MySQL to be able to reaccessed at a latter date.

Tools: Java, MySQL

REWIND Zine: Sharing stories of forgotten #WomenInSTEM

A monthly digital zine with new profile articles on a woman in STEM history. With easy to read and visual articles, I hope to share the stories of women in STEM fields from history who are often left out of the conversation in our regular school STEM classes. I especially hope young women can read these stories and feel represented and inspired to pursue STEM.

Tools: HTML, CSS, Figma
